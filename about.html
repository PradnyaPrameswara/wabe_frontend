From a Family Workshop in Gianyar

Widhi Asih Bali began as a small family workshop in Gianyar. Today, we partner with more than 100 local artisans to create pieces that carry Bali's cultural soul into contemporary spaces.

Every knot in our rattan, every carve in our teak, and every hand-finished surface reflects a story of heritage, patience, and heart. What began as a close-knit workshop has grown into a creative community rooted in craft, material honesty, and timeless design.

Balinese Soul, Modern Sensibility

We believe craftsmanship should feel timeless, but never frozen. Our collections blend Balinese artistry with modern design sensibilities so each piece feels rooted in culture while remaining relevant for contemporary homes, villas, resorts, and hospitality spaces.

From woven rattan details to hand-carved teak surfaces, every material is chosen not only for beauty, but for the texture, story, and lasting presence it brings into daily life.

Every Piece Carries Heritage and Heart

Every knot in our rattan, every carve in our teak, and every finish applied by hand reflects generations of knowledge carried forward by Bali's makers. Each object tells a story of heritage, patience, and human touch, shaped by artisans who understand both tradition and quality.

As we continue to grow, we remain guided by the same family spirit we started with: respect for our artisans, pride in honest craftsmanship, and a commitment to creating pieces with soul.
