About

See Our Story

Widhi Asih Bali began as a small family workshop in Gianyar. Today, we partner with more than 100 local artisans to create pieces that carry Bali's cultural soul into contemporary spaces.

Every knot in our rattan, every carve in our teak, and every hand-finished surface reflects a story of heritage, patience, and heart. What began as a close-knit workshop has grown into a creative community rooted in craft, material honesty, and timeless design.
